Scenario 1 — Most Common
Hard Reversal at :29
Price wicks high at exactly :29, drops fast
Path A complete — new Path B activates at next :03
Watch for bearish candle close below :29 open
CE :35 acts as magnet on the way down
Scenario 2 — Wait & See
Consolidation / Pause
Price chops between :29 node and CE :35
Algo "resting" — no clear direction yet
Wait for next :00 node to see new path
Do not force a trade in the chop zone
Scenario 3 — Less Common
Continuation Push
Price punches through :29, tags CE :35
Strong undelivered institutional order flow
:00 next hour becomes new path anchor
Fresh Path A can restart from new :00
Key rule: :29 is your ALERT zone, not a trade trigger.
Wait for price to show rejection (wick + close below) or acceptance (hold above) before acting.
